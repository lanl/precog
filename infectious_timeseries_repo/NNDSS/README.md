### **Data Cleaning**


We required at least 50 nonzero cases were observed per time series. Some dates with zero reported cases were not included in the original dataset. These dates with zero reported cases were added to the dataset. Dates that were added are denoted by NA values in the ts_dates_actual field. Data collected for 2026 were excluded as they are likely to undergo substantial revision.
