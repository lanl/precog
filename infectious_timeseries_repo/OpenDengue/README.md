\f1\b Data Cleaning
\f0\b0 \
We excluded a handful of cases reported prior to 1990 and required each location\'92s time series to have at least 52 total weeks with more than zero cases reported. We then subsetted to locations that appeared to have \'93enough\'94 nonzero cases, where \'93enough\'94 was loosely determined by the eyeball test. Interested users can go back and pull in more complete data if desired. For data from Iquitos, Peru and San Juan, Puerto Rico, reporting was not exactly weekly around Christmas and New Years. We reassigned dates to these time series to reflect a regular weekly reporting cycle. The true dates as reported in the original data are provided in the ts_dates_actual field. \
