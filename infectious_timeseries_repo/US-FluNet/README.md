### **Licensing** 


No licensing information was available. Data were made publicly available on the online dashboard. 

### **Data Cleaning**


Data included national, state-level, census region, and HHS region influenza-like illness and weighted influenza-like illness proportions from US-FluNet. In addition, WHO NREVSS data on Influenza A and B proportions were also compiled by combining the US FluNet ILI rates with the WHO NREVSS data on influenza swab positivity and sub typing. For both data sources, locations were included only if they had at least 10 observations recorded in the dataset.
