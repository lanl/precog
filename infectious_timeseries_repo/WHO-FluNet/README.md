### **Data Cleaning**


Dates correspond to the start date of the MMWR week in which data were recorded. Data were available for many different diseases and flu varieties, and we chose to include a subset of these in our cleaned data. This decision was generally made based on the amount of available data and number of locations with available data. We only included data from locations with at least 50 reported cases for a given disease and data for at least 10 weeks. For some diseases, data from specific locations were excluded due to either the apparent reporting pattern, volume of cases, etc. These were done on a case by case basis, and users interested in a more complete version of these data should explore the raw data downloaded from the WHO and included in this data release.
